In this blog post, we'll discuss a matrix equation. Consider the following matrix equation:

\[
\mathbf{A} \mathbf{X} = \mathbf{B}
\]

Where:

\[
\mathbf{A} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 3 \\
1 & 4 \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

\[
\mathbf{X} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

\[
\mathbf{B} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
8 \\
7 \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

This equation represents a linear system of equations involving matrices.
