Cong-Phat Vo - Biography

Education

  • Ph.D. in Mechanical and Automotive Engineering,
    Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Ulsan, South Korea, Feb. 2021.

  • M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering,
    Department of Mechanical Engineering, Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education, HCMC, Vietnam, Jun. 2016.

  • B.E. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering Technology,
    Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering Technology, Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education, HCMC, Vietnam, Sep. 2013.

Research interests

  • Optimal human-machine collaborative control, haptic control in teleoperation, renewable and sustainable energy system control.

Research & Work Experience

  • 2017 - present
    Graduate Researcher Fluid Power and Machine Intelligence Lab, UOU, South Korea.
    Study advanced control theories of robotics/quadrotor issues such as haptic feedback, human-robot interaction, fault-tolerant, time-delay, teleoperation.
    Participate in the proposal preparation and research on energy projects, for instance, fluid-based triboelectric nanogenerator, floating offshore wind turbines, proton-exchange membrane fuel cells.

  • 2015 - 2017
    Lecturer, Faculty of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Thu Duc College of Technology, HCMC, Vietnam.
    Teaching Modules: Microprocessor Programming, Digital technology, Circuit Design, Power Electronics, Measurement and Control technology, C/C++ Programming, Advanced control theory. Lead and coach students on scientific research.

  • 2014 - 2016
    Assistant Lecturer, University of Technology and Education, HCMC, Vietnam.
    Co-founder, Gap4Tech store, specialized supply solution technicalities.
    Graduate Researcher, Open Lab, HCMC University of Technology and Education, Vietnam. Design and develop the first version of Reception Robot.

  • 2012 - 2015
    Technician Fellow, Daewoo Royal System Vietnam Co., Ltd., HCMC, Vietnam
    Take primary responsibility for the program control of the projects, deployed all over Vietnam, such as Auto Parking Car Systems at Bitexco Financial Tower, Lim Tower, HCMC Supreme People's Court, etc. or Industrial Production Line in CSB Energy Technology Co., Ltd., GS Battery Vietnam Co., Ltd., Hyosung Viet Nam Co., Ltd.

  • 2012
    Take part in Student scientific research with a project entitled “User Interface development and Motion control for a mobile robot using Bluetooth”
